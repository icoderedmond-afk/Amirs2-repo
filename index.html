What is Blox Fruits?

Blox Fruits is one of the most popular experiences on Roblox, heavily inspired by the famous anime series One Piece. Players embark on a grand adventure across vast oceans, choosing either to become a powerful Swordsman or a formidable Fruit User.


Your main objective is to level up your character, discover rare Blox Fruits that grant superhuman abilities, defeat challenging bosses, unlock secret fighting styles, and navigate through three expansive Seas.

Blox Fruit Categories

Natural

Natural (Paramecia)

Grants unique superhuman physical abilities or environmental manipulation.

  • Popular Examples: Dough, Portal, Spirit, Venom
  • Specialty: Versatile skill sets, utility, and crowd control.
Elemental

Elemental (Logia)

Allows the user to create, control, and transform their body into natural elements.

  • Popular Examples: Light, Magma, Ice, Blizzard
  • Specialty: Passive immunity to regular NPC attacks unless enhanced with Haki. Great for grinding!
Beast

Beast (Zoan)

Allows the user to transform into mythical creatures or animals, boosting physical stats.

  • Popular Examples: Dragon, Buddha, Leopard, Phoenix
  • Specialty: High damage resistance, massive hitboxes, and durability.

Progression Across the Seas

First Sea (First Sea)

Level Req: 1 - 700

The starting ocean. Focus on learning core mechanics, purchasing your first swords/fruits, and defeating the Ice Admiral to reach the Second Sea.

Second Sea (Second Sea)

Level Req: 700 - 1500

Introduces Fruit Awakenings, Trading, Factory Raids, and Sea Events. A major power surge occurs here as you awaken abilities.

Third Sea (Third Sea)

Level Req: 1500 - 2550+

Endgame content including Sea Beasts, Leviathan, Race V4 Transformations, Cursed Dual Katana, and high-tier PvP combat.
